Old Physics Days...

Investigation of the Coupling Constants of Mesons by Using Non-Perturbative Methods:

In this research, the coupling constants of pseudoscalar and vector mesons are investigated in the framework of QCD Sum Rules at vacuum and at finite temperature. Theoretical studies on hadronic behaviours at vacuum and at finite temperature and density have a crucial role to understand experimental results and to guide future experiments. Read more... Electronic Structure Theory for Organic Battery Electrode Design:

This research focused on the design of organic radical battery electrodes using semiconductive polymer structures. The electronic structures of various polymers were analyzed, with calculations contributing to the understanding of singly occupied molecular orbitals and the potential for polyacetylene to be used as a polymer backbone in organic radical polymers.
